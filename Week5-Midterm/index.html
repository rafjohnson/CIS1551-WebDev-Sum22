Come fish with me!

Let me take you on an unforgettable fishing trip. I'll provide the kayak, fishing gear, and even lunch, and you enjoy the "fish" of your labor.

We fish winter and summer, from May through Labor Day, and pick back up when the ice is safe, generally mid-December until March.

What to Bring

  • Clothing appropriate for the weather: swimsuit or non-cotton clothing for summer, warm clothing for winter.
  • Watershoes or water-safe sandals for summer, you will get them wet, and not all launches are safe for barefeet.
  • Creepers or ice-slip-safe boots for winter.
  • Polarized sunglasses are strongly recommended, in both winter and summer.
  • A cooler to take your catch home with you
