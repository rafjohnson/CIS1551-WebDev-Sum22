Lakes We Fish

  • Lake Champlain, Mallets Bay
  • Arrowhead Mountain Lake
  • Lake Memphremagog
  • Fairfield Pond
  • Lake Carmi

The types of fish we target:

  • Largemouth Bass - the eternal favorite
  • Yellow Perch - small, but a lot of fun to catch!
  • Pike - watch out for those teeth!

When We Fish

Winter: Generally, we ice fish from December to the end of March, ice permitting. Please call or email to check the ice status.

Summer: We fish from May 15th to Labor Day.

Daily Schedule

Please be aware, times may adjust for weather, or upon request. These are just examples of what your day could look like.
We'll go over the details when you book.

Winter

  1. 0500: Meet at the boat launch
  2. 0500 - 0600: Open holes, and setup tip-ups. I'll open the holes, you'll help setup the tip-ups.
  3. 1100: I'll make lunch on the ice. Usually sausage grinders or teriyaki venison. Please let me know of any food restrictions!
  4. 1400: Begin packing up
  5. 1500: The rest of the day is yours.

Summer

  1. 0700: Meet at the boat launch
  2. 0700 - 1230: Fish from boat.
  3. 1230 - 1330:Shore Lunch! I'll cook up your catch, and we'll also have packed lunch. You won't go hungry!
  4. 1330 - 1430: A bit more shoreline fishing, or we can take the kayaks back out for a scenic paddle.
  5. 1430 and on: Enjoy the afteroon swimming, the rest of the day is yours.
